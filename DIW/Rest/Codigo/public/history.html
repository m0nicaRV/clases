Dishes that tell stories


The enormous bicentennial laurel that residents and visitors find at the entrance to Préjano gives its name to the restaurant where chef Dámaso Navajas serves. In fact, 'La Posada del Laurel' is located practically in the shadow of it, on the slopes of Peña Isasa and Peñalmonte, in an enclave with beautiful views. Calm thus invades an establishment equipped with a porch, terrace, garden and orchard, as well as a fireplace for when necessary, where vegetables, fish and meats are grilled, where wine takes center stage and where bites as simple as pan-fried snails and wood-fired peppers.
